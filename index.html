About

Goals and Vision
At our introductory meeting with you, our designers make a list of your mood board. We try to understand your need by asking questions, listening and learning about each other. We share our insights to provide you tailored made solutions of interior décor services specializing in residential, hospitality retail & commercial space. Together we focus on aesthetically pleasing, effective & elegant outcome. We spotlight is always on space, form, lighting, color, texture and pattern. Functionally we emphasize on contrast, balance, emphasis, proportion, hierarch, repetition, rhythm, pattern, white space, movement, verity and unity.
Proposal
We as per your requirements a presentation is being submitted for approval which depicts case specific features for decor and utilisation. An realistic estimate is being presented threadbare with key features for your ratification and approval. You also receive an work flow chart with timelines, specification and commercial terms and conditions.
Work begins
Now there goes the Execution process!
Once the design is being approved by our client, the detailed sectional drawings is being distributed to the site supervisors to sync with the workforce for execution in accordance with the approved designs and material specifications. Protection is taken of existing structure is well guarded to avoid any damages. Marking of fixtures, furniture’s, and electricals are done in accordance with the sectional drawings to ensure zero disparity and deviations from the agreed designs.
Project completion
A week before Handover, a complete booklet is being formed which has approved 2D and 3D drawings with a detailed checklist, warranty certificates, specifications, codifications of laminates, colour, wallpaper and fabrics, brand specification of appliances and accessories. For future services and maintenance, a procedure is also being defined for the entire space we have touched upon. The site-in-charge ensures proper sanitation and testing of every installations as a perfect picture match to the designer and our commercial team who in turn hands over the key to the client after satisfactory cross checking the entire scope of work.
